About the Workshop

A two-day workshop under IEEE Industrial Electronics Society, focused on generative AI, transformers, cloud tools, and real industrial applications through guided activities and discussion.

What you will learn

Practical foundations in machine learning, transformers, and generative models, plus how to apply them to industrial workflows.

Hands-on, not just slides

Guided labs using Google Colab, Hugging Face, and GitHub so you leave with a working mini project and a clear next step.

Industry lens

Applied sessions on responsible AI practices and a panel on current realities and future pathways in industrial automation.
