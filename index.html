About the Workshop

A two-day hands-on workshop under IEEE Industrial Electronics Society, focused on generative AI, transformers, cloud tools, and practical industrial applications. Expect guided labs, applied sessions, and an expert panel discussion.

Day 1

Date: Monday, 29 December 2025

Venue: Mehran University

  • Generative AI fundamentals and applications
  • Transformers and large language models
  • Live demonstration: language and code generation (lab)

Day 2

Date: Tuesday, 30 December 2025

Venue: Sir Syed University

  • Guided lab: build a GenAI app using Hugging Face + Google Colab
  • Responsible AI practices in industrial environments
  • Panel: AI in Industrial Automation

Who Should Attend

  • Undergraduate and graduate engineering students
  • Early-career professionals in automation, AI, and industrial electronics
  • Researchers and academics
  • IEEE members and prospective members
